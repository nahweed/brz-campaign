Scroll

Do you remember

Beating the clock just to see if you could

Racing the wind for the hell of it

Making time with nothing on the line?

Do you remember

Scraping the friction off your palms

Writing the scorecard on your knees

Daring gravity to blink first?

Or have you forgotten the

Chest-tightening

Stomach-wrenching

Teeth-chattering

Moment you felt most alive?
